## Naming
* Files: `my-class.cc` `my-class.h`
* Types: `MyClass`
* Functions `ComputeSomething`
* Variables: `snake_case`
* Constants and ENUMS: `constexpr` or `const`: use a k: `kPi`
* Namespaces: `snake_case`
* Macros: `CAPS_LOCK`

Abbreviations: `StartRpc`, not `StartRPC`
